01 — ABOUT

Where I've
looked for signal.

I'm a Data Analyst with an MSc in Data Science & Analytics from the University of Hertfordshire, and just under five years turning raw, inconsistent data into things people can actually act on.

That's meant building machine learning models to detect glucose levels from near-infrared voltage signals in a medical device, untangling customer transaction data to catch fraud and churn for a US fintech client, and — most recently — building the data backbone for a pharmaceutical export business expanding into Amazon and eBay.

What ties it together: I'm comfortable being the person who cleans the mess before anyone else can use it, and the person who explains what it means once it's clean.
